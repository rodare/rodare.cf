Residence permit extension

Aug 17, 2023

I submitted my application for the extension of my residence permit on April 3rd 2023. It may take up to 6 months to receive the result, timeframe which I communicated during the Exist scholarship application. I received the decision earlier (on 19th July 2023). However, I found a little error on it because it said that I should work for a “minimum of 20 hours per week”, ... while the previous decision said “no more than 20 hours per week”. Link to the previous decision ... I found that this error came from the application files that were filled by FDK, because they forgot to write "no more than", and simply wrote "20 hours". So I requested FDK to send me a corrected version of those files as soon as possible, to comply with the appellation period of 14 days since the received decision.

The government officer told me that it didn't matter because there is no requirement to specify a number of hours on my type of contract: ... “Contract for a specific activity” ( “umowa o dzieło” in polish) is a type of contract that only requires: 1) the activity to perform, 2) the salary and 3) the completion date. so my employer (FDK) can write any number of hours and it won't make a difference. Despite of this, I decided to request a correction of the decision ... by submitting the corrected files (a copy of which I sent to Mrs. Patzer) and paying the fee of 220 zl involved in the process just to have the decision changed from: "minimum of 20 hours per week" to "no more than 20 hours per week". Although of course, it could have been any number of hours, just to avoid confusion for the EXIST, ... which sets a maximum of 5 hours of work outside the EXIST program. With this requirement I also comply thanks to the letter I received from FDK (a copy of which I sent during the EXIST application and later via email to Mrs. Patzer) Now I need to wait for about 2 months to receive the corrected decision.
