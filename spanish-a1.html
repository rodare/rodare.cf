Level A1

This is the most basic level of mastery of a language. At this level the speaker is expected to be able to interact easily with others who make an effort to speak slowly and clearly, not in a natural conversational rhythm. The person is expected to be able to carry out basic conversations such as introducing himself, asking and answering questions about personal data (name, where he lives, hobbies). The vocabulary is basic, and is limited to concrete nouns and close to the day (domestic animals, parts of the house, food, parts of the body).
